About

I am Abu Naser Niloy. I was involved in active politics of Bangladesh Student's League for the last 10 years. I am a Primary Member of Bangladesh Student's League, Naogaon District Unit since 12/12/12 and I was a Former Assistant Secretary of Bangladesh Student's League, Naogaon District Unit (2015-2017). I joined politics when i realized that a righteous political leadership is required to change the fate of the underdeveloped and disregarded Naogaon region. At present i am serving as a activist of Bangladesh Student's League from Naogaon Sadar.
